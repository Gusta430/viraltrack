How to get on Spotify playlists
Quick guide — tap to expand
1. Spotify for Artists (editorial playlists)
Pitch your song at least 7 days before release (ideally 2-4 weeks). Go to Spotify for Artists → Music → Pitch a song. Fill in genre, mood, and a short story about the track. This is the only way to reach playlists like New Music Friday and RapCaviar.
2. Algorithmic playlists
Release Radar, Discover Weekly and Radio are driven by data. Get listeners to save your track and add it to their own playlists — this signals quality to the algorithm. The first 24-48 hours matter most.
3. Independent curators
Use SubmitHub or Groover to reach playlist curators. You can also DM curators directly on Instagram. Avoid services that guarantee placement for money — these often break Spotify's rules.
4. Build your foundation
Get followers on your Spotify artist profile, promote pre-saves before release, and share actively in the first 48 hours. Collaborate with other artists for cross-promotion. ViralTrack generates all of this for you in the promo plan.
